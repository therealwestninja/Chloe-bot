Chloe Solo

A standalone, in-browser AI companion built on the Chloe cognitive engine — facts, insights, episodic and semantic memory, reflection, and feedback learning, all running locally with no server and no account. On a Perchance page she speaks through the free ai-text and image plugins; opened on its own she runs in demo mode so you can see the machine work.
Open Her mind at any time to watch her think, and slow the pace down to read along.
